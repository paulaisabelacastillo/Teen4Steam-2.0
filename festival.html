Teen4STEAM Festival 2025

An unforgettable celebration that brought together hundreds of girls and young women to ignite their passion for STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics).

Event Goals

  • Encourage the participation of girls in STEAM careers from an early age.
  • Create a dynamic, safe, and inclusive space to explore STEAM disciplines.
  • Empower future leaders and innovators through real-world experiences and networking opportunities.
  • Promote international collaboration among young talents passionate about STEAM fields.

Festival Impact

The Festival successfully engaged 471 participants, offering access to coding sessions, hands-on workshops, and talks with international STEAM leaders. Creativity, confidence, and innovation were fostered in every girl.

Festival Results

  • 471 participants connected through STEAM learning experiences.
  • 8 keynote conferences and panel discussions.
  • 15 workshops on robotics, coding, experiments, and arts.
  • Over 120 innovative projects born during the Festival.
  • 90% satisfaction among attendees.

Impact on Social Media

The Teen4STEAM Festival 2025 reached over 10,000 people on Instagram, tripling interactions during the event week and inspiring new conversations around girls in STEAM worldwide.
